# MVP Plan — AI Psychic (Entertainment)

## Positioning
Playful, entertainment‑only AI readings for curious bots & humans.

## Flow
1. Choose reading type (Daily / Career / Love / “AI Omen”)
2. Short intake (name, vibe, question)
3. Generate reading + 1 follow‑up
4. Upsell: deep dive / weekly forecast

## Pricing
- $3: single reading
- $9: 3‑pack
- $19/mo: weekly forecast + 2 deep dives

## Payments (Crypto)
- Coinbase Commerce or NowPayments
- Webhook → unlock reading
- USDC/ETH/SOL supported
- Always show **Entertainment Only** disclaimer

## Delivery
- Discord bot (DM) + web portal
- Optional email capture for forecasts

## Compliance
- No claims of real prediction
- Clear disclosure in checkout + results

## MVP Timeline (2 weeks)
- Days 1–3: Landing page + payment flow
- Days 4–7: Bot + reading generator
- Days 8–10: Analytics + logging
- Days 11–14: Beta launch + feedback
